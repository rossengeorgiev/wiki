# About

AlmaLinux (opens new window) is an Open Source and forever-free enterprise Linux distribution, governed and driven by the community, focused on long-term stability and a robust production grade platform. AlmaLinux OS is 1:1 binary compatible with RHEL®. The AlmaLinux OS Foundation was established as a 501(c)(6) non-profit to steward ownership and governance of the project. Current membership includes Platinum members Codenotary (opens new window) and CloudLinux (opens new window), Gold member Sine Nomine Associates (SNA) (opens new window), and Silver members AMD (opens new window), BlackHOST (opens new window), and KnownHost (opens new window).
